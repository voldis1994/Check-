Put your ORIGINAL MetaTrader 4 folder in this directory.

Example:
  Check\template\MetaTrader 4\terminal.exe
  Check\template\MetaTrader 4\metaeditor.exe
  Check\template\MetaTrader 4\MQL4\...

Then run:
  Check\SETUP.bat

SETUP will:
  - copy this MT4 into instances\_master
  - install CHECK.mq4
  - clone a private MT4 per account when you add one
  - open the CHECK desk EXE
